Find. Book. Study.

Baithak is a smart library seat booking and occupancy management platform that helps students reserve seats, view real-time availability, and eliminate ghost bookings using QR-based verification.

Book a Seat

Why Baithak?

📍 Real-Time Seat Map

View available and occupied seats instantly before reaching the library.

📱 QR Check-In

Each seat has a unique QR code to verify actual occupancy and prevent fake reservations.

⏳ Auto Release

Seats are automatically released if users fail to check in or remain absent for too long.

👥 Group Study Rooms

Reserve collaborative study spaces for projects, discussions, and group learning.

🔇 Quiet Zones

Dedicated silent study areas for students requiring maximum concentration.

🔐 Secure Login

College email authentication ensures secure access for verified students.

How It Works

1. Check Availability

View live library seat status remotely.

2. Reserve Seat

Select and book an available seat instantly.

3. Scan QR Code

Verify occupancy when you arrive.

4. Study Efficiently

Unused seats are automatically reallocated.

Future Scope

🤖 AI-Powered Occupancy Detection

Future versions of Baithak will integrate YOLO and OpenCV for privacy-first occupancy monitoring without storing facial data, making seat utilization even smarter and more accurate.
